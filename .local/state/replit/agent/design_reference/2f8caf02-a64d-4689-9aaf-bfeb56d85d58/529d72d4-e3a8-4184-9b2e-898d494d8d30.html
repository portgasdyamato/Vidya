Powerful Accessibility Features

Our AI-powered platform transforms traditional learning materials into accessible formats for all students.

Document Processing

Upload PDFs and DOCX files to extract text and convert to high-quality audio. Perfect for reading assignments and study materials.

Image Recognition

Take photos of textbook pages and get detailed descriptions of images, diagrams, and charts using GPT-4 Vision API.

Video Transcription

Paste YouTube links or video URLs to get accurate transcriptions using Whisper API, making lectures accessible to deaf students.

AI Summarization

Get concise summaries of complex materials and simplified explanations of difficult concepts using GPT-4's advanced understanding.

Text-to-Speech

Convert any processed text into natural-sounding audio with multiple voice options for an enhanced listening experience.

Interactive Quizzes

Generate customized quizzes from your study materials to test understanding and reinforce learning objectives.
